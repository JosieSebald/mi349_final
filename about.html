Hello, my name is Josie Sebald. I'm an artist who is striving to work in animation at Disney or Pixar. I'm currently a junior attending Michigan State University and pursuing a degree in Games and Interactive Media.
I've been drawing for as long as I can remember, making it one of my favorite hobbies. I am skilled in pencil drawing, 3D modeling, and 2D digital art, but I mostly focus on 2D digital art. I am also currently open for commission requests!
